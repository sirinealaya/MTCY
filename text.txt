Dogs:
Dogs  are domesticated mammals, not natural wild animals. 
They were originally bred from wolves.
 They have been bred by humans for a long time
CAT:
A cat is a furry animal that has a long tail and sharp claws. Cats are often kept as pets.
BIRD:
a bird is a warm blooded member of the Aves class, which has feathers, a beak, no teeth, wings and bears young in eggs.
Giraffe:
A giraffe  is a mammal which lives in Africa. Giraffes have an even number of toes and are the tallest land animals.
